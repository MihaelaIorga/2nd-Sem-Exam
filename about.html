ABOUT

Most active promoter of the tropical and global bass scene in Hungary, host of the Tropical Heat radio show at Budapest’s Tilos Radio and founder of Babylon Records delivering forward thinking music and artists worldwide.

With his diverse bass heavy productions he gained the support of artists like Congorock, Branko, Riff Raff, Ookay and more. During the summer of 2015 he performed on Fusion and Ozora festival and did his first international tour in Germany, France, Czech Republic, Poland, Lithuania and Belarus followed by his second album "Full Moon".

In 2016 he toured in Mexico, Brasil and played at Feel festival Germany and EPHEMERE festival Tunis. When not on tour, he organizes Budapest’s monthly global / tropical bass event called BWOLA.

For any kind of collaborations use email: stanislav@babylonrec.com
